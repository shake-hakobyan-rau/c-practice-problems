# Problem 1

**Statement:**

In a store, you have two products: milk and chocolate. Given their prices (0 < sum <= 1000), quantities (0 < count <= 10), and the total amount (0 < price <= 1000), calculate the change from the purchase.

**Input:**

100 500 2 1 5000 

**Output:**

4300
